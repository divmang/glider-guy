🪂 Glider Guy

A fast-paced mobile game by Salt Wolf Games

About the Game

Glider Guy is a skill-based mobile browser game where you pilot a hang-glider through a series of obstacles. Air guns on the ground fire bursts of air upward — tap them to stay airborne. Each gun holds 3 shots before it needs to reload. Fly through gaps in the green pillars to score points. The game gets faster and harder as your score increases.

Compete on the global leaderboard and see how you rank against players from around the world.

About Salt Wolf Games

Salt Wolf Games is an independent game studio focused on creating fun, accessible mobile gaming experiences. Glider Guy is our first title.
